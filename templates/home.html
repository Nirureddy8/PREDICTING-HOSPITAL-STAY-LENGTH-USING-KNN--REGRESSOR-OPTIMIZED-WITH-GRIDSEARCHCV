About

Predicting Hospital Stay Duration for Better Resource Management

Overview

It focuses on predicting the duration of hospital stays for patients based on various factors such as demographics, medical history, and current condition. By utilizing advanced machine learning models, healthcare providers can optimize resource allocation, reduce costs, and enhance patient care. This system aims to provide accurate and actionable predictions to aid in effective decision-making.

Techniques

K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN)

The KNN algorithm is used for classification, leveraging its simplicity and effectiveness to predict outcomes based on proximity to similar data points.

Grid Search Optimization

Grid Search is employed to find the optimal hyperparameters for the machine learning model, ensuring maximum accuracy and performance.
